About JIT Mart

Serving Juba Since 2006

JIT Mart has grown from a single storefront to one of the largest supermarket chains in Juba and South Sudan. Our Juba Town Branch brings quality retail experiences to every neighborhood.

Our mission is to provide exceptional shopping experiences with quality products at fair prices. We're more than a store — we're a community partner committed to the growth and prosperity of South Sudan.
