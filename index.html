FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

Yes! We offer competitive wholesale pricing for bulk orders across all departments. Contact us on WhatsApp or call us to discuss your requirements and get a custom quote.

What areas do you deliver to?

We provide delivery across Juba and surrounding areas for bulk purchases. Call us on +211 959 336 699 or message us on WhatsApp to confirm delivery to your specific location and arrange a time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

  • Cash (South Sudanese Pound & USD)
  • Mobile money transfers
  • Bank transfers for large orders

Contact us for details on payment for your specific purchase.

How can I check if a specific product is in stock?

The quickest way is to send us a WhatsApp message or call us directly. Our staff can check availability and give you current pricing instantly. You can also visit any of our branches — we're open every day from 8:00 AM to 6:30 PM.

Can I place an order in advance?

Absolutely. For large or bulk orders we encourage you to call or WhatsApp us in advance so we can prepare your items and ensure everything is ready when you arrive or arrange delivery at your preferred time.
